Pitch

SUPER ICK is a a cartoony mix of peaceful fishing and chaotic bullet hell action, with dozens of fish to collect and over twenty planets to discover.

Description

In SUPER ICK, fill the shoes of a weary fisherman journeying to a far-off galaxy to collect and document all of its fish before a mysterious disease wipes them all out. You'll travel from planet to planet in whatever order your choose, fishing in their lakes and ponds, and fighting deadly aliens on the trips between them. By selling the fish you catch, you'll be able to buy upgrades and powerups for your ship to make traversing the cosmos more manageable.

- 21 planets to discover
- 63 fish to collect, each with its own description
- Unique, cartoonish art style and characters
- Small open galaxy to explore and one's leisure
- 2+ hours of gameplay
